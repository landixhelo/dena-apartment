Making Your Stay Comfortable

Set 1.8 km from Vlore Beach and 2 km from Independence Square in Vlorë, Dena Apartment offers accommodation with a kitchenette. The accommodation is 1 km from the Vjetër Beach and has free WiFithroughout the property.

Kuzum Baba is 2.3 km from the apartment. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 150 km from Dena Apartment.
